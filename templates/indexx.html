    A LITTLE ABOUT PICSHAKE

    PicShake is a mobile app that lets you share pictures with your friends and family using simple phone shake gestures

    We believe that sharing pictures should always be fun and easy. PicShake shifts the responsibility of sending pictures from one person, to the whole group receiving those pictures. With our app, when a picture is taken it is uploaded to the cloud with a password you pick and your friends within 500 meters can download the picture directly to their phone by using that password. You do not need to create a group, tag people online, or even have their contact information. Your friends get the picture without you doing all the work. This will change the whole photo sharing experience.

    One other thing that makes PicShake unique is that most of the photo sharing apps on the market degrade the picture quality when it is shared. However, PicShake lets you share full size high quality pictures. Whatâ€™s really neat about picshake is that if you upload several pictures with the same password and within the same range, you are essentially creating a private gallery. For instance you upload a picture and then your friend uploads another one with the same password. These two pictures get put together in a single gallery.

SECURITY!

We have taken alot of security measures to make the app secure and protect your pictures. Photos can be downloaded only by people who know the passcode and who are located within 500 meters from the sender. Communication with servers is encreypted and encoded. Pictures are automatically erased and deleted from the server within 24 hours. which means that users can only download the shared pictures within 24 hours from the upload time.
